Community Outreach & Leadership

My work extends beyond projects and classrooms into communities that spark curiosity, uplift voices, and create space for meaningful growth. I believe knowledge expands when shared like any good universe does and I try to contribute to ecosystems that empower others the way they empowered me.

Google Developer Groups – IIT Mandi (On-Campus Organizer)

At GDG IIT Mandi, I help create a campus environment where students experiment, build, and learn together. From workshops to tech talks to hands-on sessions, I enjoy bringing people into the world of development and watching ideas collide, merge, and transform.

AnitaB India – AIP Scholar & Mentee

Being part of the AnitaB community introduced me to powerful networks of women in tech. Through mentorship and AIP sessions, I learned the importance of lifting others as I grow and the strength that comes from shared journeys across technology and leadership.

SheFi Scholar – Web3 & Decentralized Finance

As a SheFi Scholar, I explored the intersection of Web3, technology, and financial inclusion. It became a space where curiosity met innovation, and global conversations shaped my understanding of decentralization and digital empowerment.

Rising Star – Rewriting the Code (RTC)

Selected as an RTC Rising Star, I joined a global network of women driven by purpose, resilience, and ambition. It taught me that community isn’t just about belonging — it’s about building pathways for others to rise too.

We Scholar – Harvard WE Code

As a WE Scholar, I engaged with one of the most inspiring communities in computing. The experience encouraged me to think boldly, collaborate openly, and align my journey with impact-driven leadership.
