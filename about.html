ABOUT ME: “The Gopika Origin Episode”

Hi, I’m Gopika currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering at IIT Mandi, after an early education phase defined by curiosity, too many questions, and an unusual excitement for problem-solving that most teachers politely called “enthusiastic.”

My journey into tech didn’t start with a plan. It started with… an intellectual detour, followed by a curiosity explosion, followed by a long series of experiments that may or may not have crashed my system. (Scientists now call this sequence The Gopika Big Bang.)

Since then, my interests have settled into a stable orbit around:

  • AI/ML : modelling patterns in a universe full of delightful randomness
  • AR/VR & Human-centred systems : designing experiences powered by empathy, not ego
  • Cloud & scalable tech : where ideas expand faster than the observable cosmos
  • Tech exploration : because the unknown is the best playground

What drives me is something I call Tech Empathy the belief that technology becomes meaningful only when it understands people, supports them, and solves problems that actually matter. That philosophy quietly threads through everything I create (you’ll see proof in the Projects section).

I’m also running a continuous self-upgrade cycle: refining skills, improving clarity, debugging insecurities, and optimising for growth. The goal? A version of me that is more stable, more curious, and far less likely to crash under emotional multithreading.

In short: I’m building a life and career rooted in logic, empathy, exploration, and just the right amount of scientific drama.

Welcome to my universe where curiosity expands freely, ideas collide creatively, and everything begins with a spark.
